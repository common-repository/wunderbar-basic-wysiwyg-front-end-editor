Quick Reference Guide

Any editable content on the current page of your website will be designated by stylized crosshairs near its upper left corner. When you click the crosshairs, they will be replaced by a red dotted outline which marks the limits of what you can edit. Simultaneously, at the top of the page, Wunderbar Basic's formatting buttons will appear. These work like to a word processor to style and save your edits.

Press the SAVE button at the far upper left when you're done editing. Prior versions of your content are always saved, and you can revisit them by clicking the down-arrow. Once loaded, any version can become current by clicking SAVE. Wunderbar's formatting buttons are listed below, grouped by function and described from left to right:

Boldface and Italics

Bullet points, numbered lists, indented and outdented text, quoted text, text alignment

Heading and paragraph styles, font overrides, size overrides, line height

Font color, background color, copy & remove formatting

Create/edit hyperlink, remove hyperlink, create/edit anchor point.

Create/edit table, insert horizontal rule, insert special characters, create/edit DIV

Show invisibles, find, paste special, view/edit HTML source
